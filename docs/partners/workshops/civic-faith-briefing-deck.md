## What Is the J-Bay Community Hub?

A mobile app for Jeffreys Bay residents to:

- **Find local programs** — education classes, surf sessions, youth events, volunteering
- **Connect with trusted organisations** — Joshua Project, Wave Point, Victory Church, and more
- **Verify your residency** — one-time secure check to access resident-only listings
- **No cost to users** — community treasury covers transaction fees

## Why Does J-Bay Need This?

Many residents don't know what programs are available in their own town. Information is scattered across WhatsApp groups, flyers, and word of mouth.

The Community Hub brings everything into one simple app:

> "What education programs are running this month?"
> "Where can my teenager go this weekend?"
> "How do I volunteer at the community garden?"

All answered in one place.

## How It Works (Simple Version)

1. **Download the app** (TestFlight for iPhone, coming to Android)
2. **Verify you're a J-Bay resident** (simple one-time check)
3. **Browse listings** by category: Education, Recreation, Entertainment
4. **Connect directly** with program organisers
5. **No personal data shared publicly** — your identity stays private

## Who's Behind It

- Built by **InkPixel**, a South African tech initiative
- Funded through **Project Catalyst** (Cardano community grants)
- Guided by local partners: Joshua Project, Wave Point Foundation, Victory Church, and others
- **Open source** — the technology is free for any community to use

## Privacy & Safety

- Your personal information is **never shared publicly**
- Identity verification uses **privacy-preserving technology** (zero-knowledge proofs)
- Organisations only see what you choose to share
- All youth programs have **safeguarding policies** in place

## How Faith Leaders Can Help

1. **Spread the word** — mention the app to your congregation / community
2. **List your programs** — if your organisation offers classes, events, or services
3. **Provide feedback** — help us make the app useful for J-Bay
