# Workshop Template

**Date:** TBD (schedule via partner contact)
**Format:** In-person / Video call

## Agenda

1. **Introductions** — who's present, what they hope to get from the platform
2. **Platform demo** — show listing browse, explain privacy model at high level
3. **Listing taxonomy session** — what offers/services this org would list
   - What categories fit?
   - What proof requirements make sense?
   - What cadence (one-time, weekly, seasonal)?
4. **Pain points** — what's broken today (paper-based, word of mouth, no discoverability)
5. **Next steps** — what the org needs to onboard, who the liaison is
6. **Follow-up** — doc link shared, taxonomy update committed

## Expected Output

- Workshop notes → `docs/partners/workshops/YYYY-MM-DD-ORG.md`
- Taxonomy updates → `docs/partners/listing-taxonomy.md`
- Seed listing data (if org ready to commit) → `docs/partners/seed-data/ORG.json`

## Before the Workshop

- Review current `docs/partners/listing-taxonomy.md` for existing categories
- Have the app running on device to demo (TestFlight or Expo Go)
- Prepare a 1-sentence privacy pitch: "Your data never leaves your phone — only proofs do."
