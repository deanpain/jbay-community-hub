## Partner Context

Victory Church Frontline Youth runs event programming and youth development in Jeffreys Bay. Their focus includes entertainment events, youth cultural activities, and Gap Year leadership programs. They collaborate with Victory Gap Year for entrepreneurship/life skills labs.

## Proposed Listing Categories

### 1. Youth Cultural Events
- Ticketed events with privacy-preserving attendance proofs
- Music, arts, drama, and cultural showcases
- Age-specific: teen nights, young adult events
- Proof requirements: age verification (13+, 18+ as applicable)

### 2. Gap Year Leadership Labs
- Entrepreneurship / life skills workshops (Victory Gap Year)
- Curriculum collaboration with Joshua Project for AET pathways
- Structured programs with mentorship
- Proof requirements: enrollment confirmation, ID verification

### 3. Youth Safety Programs
- Safe space guidelines and reporting mechanisms
- Buddy system for events
- Night-time event security protocols

## Moderation Notes

- Age-restricted events require verified age credential (privacy-preserving via identity-worker)
- Ticketing metadata must not expose participant identities publicly
- Gap Year programs may have cost/registration — clearly displayed in listing
- All youth events must have designated safeguarding lead on-site
- Event capacity limits must be enforced through booking system
