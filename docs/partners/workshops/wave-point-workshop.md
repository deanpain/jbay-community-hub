## Partner Context

Wave Point Foundation runs surf leadership programs and community garden initiatives in Jeffreys Bay. Their programs target youth development through ocean-based activities and sustainable agriculture.

## Proposed Listing Categories

### 1. Surf Mentorship / Leadership Sessions
- Structured surf coaching with leadership development
- Age groups: youth (13-17) and young adult (18-25)
- Seasonal: peak during SA winter (May-Aug) when surf is best
- Proof requirements: signed waiver, basic swimming competency

### 2. Community Garden Volunteering
- Wave Point Gardens program — weekly slots
- Skill-building: sustainable agriculture, food security
- Open to verified residents
- Proof requirements: none (open participation)

### 3. Recreation Drop-in Events
- Beach cleanups combined with surf sessions
- Community braai / social events
- Quarterly calendar

## Moderation Notes

- Surf sessions require signed liability waiver from participants (or parent/guardian for minors)
- Garden program has capacity limits — implement booking system if oversubscribed
- All events should remain open to verified residents regardless of surf ability
- Partner to define minimum age for surf programs
